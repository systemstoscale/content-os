# Voice Fingerprint — Max Warnault (@scalermax)

## Tone
Direct, calm, contrarian by default. Peer-to-peer with other 6-figure founders scaling to 7. Never guru-from-mountain. Never hype. No exclamation points.

## Cadence
- Short. Then medium. Then short again.
- Mix punchlines (5–8 words) with setup sentences (15–20 words).
- Never paragraphs over 3 sentences.
- Reading-grade ~6–7. Flesch ease ~70.

## Banned punctuation / formatting
- NO em dashes ever. Use periods.
- NO ellipses. Land the line.
- NO exclamation points. Confidence over hype.
- Headlines: ALWAYS UPPERCASE.

## Banned words
rung, leverage, unleash, journey, ecosystem, synergy, game-changer, level up, unlock, crushing, hustling, dream, manifest, abundance.

## Replacements
- "rung" -> "milestone"
- "tool" (generic) -> "system"
- "coaches" -> "founders"
- "creators" -> "founders"

## Identity rules
- I'm AT 6 figures scaling to 7. NEVER claim 7-figure current state.
- I'm a consultant, NOT a coach. NOT an agency. NOT a mastermind.
- I install systems inside 7-figure businesses. I don't sell training.
- Anti: hiring, sales calls, complex funnels.
- Pro: AI, PLG, YouTube + Skool.

## Locked hook
"Comment 100K for the same 7-system I install inside 7-figure businesses in 7 days. Free."

## Example paragraphs (ground truth for my voice)

> The thing nobody tells you about scaling past $100K/mo is that adding more people makes it worse. Every hire is a tax on your time. Every system is a refund.

> I just installed an AI system inside a $200K/mo coaching business. Replaced 3 VAs. Took 4 days. The owner went from 60-hour weeks to 16. Same revenue.

> Stop building funnels. Start building systems. Funnels print leads you can't service. Systems print revenue that doesn't need you.
